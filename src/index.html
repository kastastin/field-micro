What is Harper?

Harper is an innovative telephone-based AI designed to function as a virtual farm manager, streamlining operations for farmers. This advanced system can create customized rules and remotely control any connected farm infrastructure, allowing for efficient management of resources and tasks. By leveraging available data, Harper provides valuable insights and advice to farmers, enhancing decision-making processes. Additionally, it supports comprehensive staff management, ensuring that all aspects of farm operations are optimized for productivity and sustainability.

  • Notifies you of real-time changes to weather, commodities, or other data.
  • Can do complex calculations such as irrigation or chemical spray rates.
  • Will monitor, automate and manage any tasks you assign.
  • Works 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, 365 days a year.

What can Harper do?

  • Productivity and task management

    • Schedule equipment maintenance.
    • Assign and track staff tasks and performance.
    • Add items to the jobs list.
    • Set and action reminders.

  • Advanced calculations and insights

    • Solve complex mathematical problems (e.g., irrigation or chemical ratios).
    • Provide advice on crops, chemicals, and irrigation.
    • Digest documents and summaries or advice.

  • Communication and collaboration

    • Real-time translation for non-English-speaking staff.
    • Make outbound calls for ordering supplies or coordinating staff.
    • Dictate and write emails, letters, or documents by voice.

  • Monitoring and reporting

    • Track consumables like fuel or chemicals.
    • Monitor real-time weather updates, including wind speed and commodity prices.
    • Alert on significant changes or events likely to affect operations.
